The year is Epoch 4237.

For around 70 years, a spacefaring Terran Space Commune has been a member of the Friendship Accord, alongside three fellow spacefaring nations- the Khell'zhak State, the Bohoy Civil Union, and the Indori Serene Nation.

Many thousands of years ago, the Galaxy was connected by a network of tunnels through a dimension beneath normalspace; witchspace. These witchlanes allowed easy traversal, making a years-long journey into weeks. Yet, time was not kind, and the Lanes decayed. Today, they cover about 400 square light-years, and around four thousand star systems.

Many of these star systems are rugged and uninhabitable.

Yet in many, there is an abundance of life, of civilisation, and of joy.
