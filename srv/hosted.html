services hosted on calicocore

hi there!

calicocore hosts a collection of super cool + useful stuff, all run under a subdomain of calicocore.space. currently, we have a nextcloud, and a matrix, and soon a fedi instance! these services are run by mia (hi!) and primarily focussed on creating a haven for trans, queer, and disabled people as the 'net gets less friendly.

drive

a nextcloud instance. currently offering for free 50GB of cloud storage, a Markdown notetaking tool, a shareable, open calendar, a photo gallery, web bookmark storage, and, some organisation and collaboration tools.

Matrix and Fediverse

an opensource communication system!
